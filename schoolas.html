About BTEC

The BTEC system, or the "business and Technology Education Council",
represents an international standard in professional education and provides certificates and diplomas in a variety of specialties. This system allows students to combine theoretical study with practical application, which prepares its graduates for real work requirements and increases their chances of finding a suitable job or continuing their higher education.

Major

IT

IT Students Study Technical Education and the subjects they study are Unit: Data and Spreadsheet Modelling , Unit: Data base , Unit: Web Development and Unit: Mobile Application Development.

Engineering

engineering Students Study Technical Education and the subjects they study are Unit: Introduction to Engineering , Unit: Engineering Design, Unit: Engineering Materials: and Unit: Engineering Mathematics.

Business

Business Students Study Technical Education and the subjects they study are Unit: Online business, Unit: Promoting and branding , Unit: Supply chain and Unit: Business ethics.

Art&Design

Art& Design Students Study Technical Education and the subjects they study are Unit : Introduction to Art and Design, Unit :Developing Art and Design Skills , Unit : Digital Art and Design and Unit: Art and Design Contextual Studies.
